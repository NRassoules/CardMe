CardMe

About Us

Nearly 5,000 young people die every year as a result of underage drinking. We can bring that number to zero. By crowdsourcing a collection of data about carding practices at bars, CardMe empowers communities to eliminate underage drinking. On a micro level, this enables consumers to make responsible decisions about which establishments they patronize. On a macro level, the database which CardMe establishes can be used to analyze trends and identify patterns, and inform policy decisions.

Created by Kim Asenbeck, Brenna Carver, Djassi Julien, and Nikolas Rassoules
